Introduction
This Privacy Policy applies to the game Pop Word. We value your privacy and want to be transparent about how we handle your information.

Data Collection and Use
Pop Word does not directly collect, store, or share personal data with the developer.
The game does not use advertising, third-party logins, external analytics, leaderboards, or cloud save features.

However, to function on the Meta Quest platform, the system may rely on basic Meta Quest platform services which process certain technical data:

Platform Analytics
Meta may provide anonymized, aggregated analytics to the developer through the Developer Dashboard. This includes:

Number of unique users

Device types (e.g., Quest 2, Quest 3) and OS versions

Regional distribution (country level)

Session duration and performance metrics

These reports are strictly for performance monitoring and do not contain personally identifiable information.

Internet Access and Permissions
While Pop Word is designed to be played primarily offline, internet connectivity may be used by the Meta Quest system for standard platform updates or basic analytics reporting as described above. No personal data is transmitted to the developer's own servers or to third parties outside of Meta.

Data Deletion Requests
Because Pop Word does not store personal data or user accounts on private servers, there is no developer-side database from which to delete information.
